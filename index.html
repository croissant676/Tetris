Tetris

This game is a recreation of the popular retro game Tetris.
The goal of the game is to score as many points as possible
by clearing lines.
The game ends when the blocks reach the top of the screen.

You can change your controls by clicking the buttons below.

To pause the game, press the ESC key.

To restart the game, press the F4 key.
Try to beat 20000 lines.
